# General information

The 2023 Odisha train collision was a major rail accident that occurred on 2 June 2023, in Balasore district, Odisha state, India.

# Location

The 2023 Odisha train collision occurred in Balasore district in the east Indian state of Odisha[1].

# Date and Time

The 2023 Odisha train collision occurred on 2 June 2023, around 19:00 IST (14:30 UTC) near Bahanaga Bazar railway station on the Howrah-Chennai main line
