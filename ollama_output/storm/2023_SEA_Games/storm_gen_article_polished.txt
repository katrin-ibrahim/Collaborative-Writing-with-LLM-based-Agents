# Overview of 2023 Southeast Asian Games

The 2023 Southeast Asian Games, also known as the SEA Games 2023, took place in a designated location from May 5 to May 17, 2023.[1][2] This edition marked significant advancements in sustainable practices and energy integration within sports venues.[3]

## Venue Management

The planning process for venue management during the 2023 SEA Games involved extensive coordination and strategic planning. Major challenges included logistical issues, crowd control, and ensuring safety standards were met throughout the event.[1][3] Mitigation strategies were implemented to address common issues such as infrastructure readiness and efficient resource utilization.[1]

### Renewable Energy Integration

A notable feature of the 2023 SEA Games was its emphasis on renewable energy sources. Venues incorporated solar panels and wind turbines, aiming for a reduced carbon footprint during the event.[1][3] This effort to integrate sustainable practices also included waste management systems designed to minimize environmental impact.[3]

### Green Technologies

The implementation of green technologies was another key aspect of the 2023 SEA Games. Venues were equipped with energy-efficient lighting and ventilation systems, while digital platforms reduced paper usage.[1][2] These measures reflected a commitment to sustainability in sports management.

## Sports Competitions

Key sports featured in the 2023 Southeast Asian Games included athletics, swimming, badminton, and football among others. A wide range of events provided a comprehensive showcase of athletic talent across different disciplines from various Southeast Asian nations.[1]

### Athlete Preparation

Athletes faced numerous challenges during their preparation for the 2023 SEA Games, including rigorous training regimens and psychological preparation to cope with competitive pressures.[1] Coping strategies were developed to support athletes in managing stress and maintaining peak performance levels.[1]
