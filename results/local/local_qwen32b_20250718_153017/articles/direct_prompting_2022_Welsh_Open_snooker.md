# 2022_Welsh_Open_(snooker)

The 2022 Welsh Open was a professional snooker tournament held from February 14 to 20, 2022, at the Celtic Manor Resort in Newport, Wales. It marked the 39th edition of the Welsh Open, one of the most prestigious ranking events on the World Snooker Tour (WST). The tournament featured a field of 128 players competing for a total prize fund of £600,000, with the winner taking home £100,000.

## Background

The Welsh Open has been a part of the WST since its inception in 1987, with the event initially being known as the Benson & Hedges Welsh Masters until 1996. It was renamed the Welsh Open in 1997 and has since become a key fixture on the snooker calendar, attracting top players from around the world. The tournament is particularly significant for Welsh players, as it offers them a chance to compete in front of their home crowd and potentially claim the title of Welsh Open champion.

## Venue

The 2022 Welsh Open was hosted by the Celtic Manor Resort, a luxury hotel and golf resort located in Newport, Wales. The venue has been a regular host of the tournament since 2011, providing a modern and well-equipped setting for the competition. The main playing area, the Spa Suite, offered a comfortable and visually appealing environment for both players and spectators alike.

## Format

The 2022 Welsh Open followed a standard ranking event format, consisting of six rounds of matches:

### Qualifying Rounds
The qualifying rounds were held at the Marshall Arena in Sheffield, England, from January 17 to 20, 2022. Sixty-four players competed in these rounds, with the winners advancing to the main event in Newport.

### Main Event
The main event in Newport comprised five rounds of matches, including:
- **First Round**: 64 players
- **Second Round**: 32 players
- **Third Round**: 16 players
- **Quarterfinals**: 8 players
- **Semifinals**: 4 players
- **Final**: 2 players

All matches were played over the best-of-11 frames, except for the final, which was contested over the best-of-19 frames.

## Final

The final match of the 2022 Welsh Open saw a thrilling contest between Kyren Wilson and Zhao Xintong. Both players had demonstrated exceptional form throughout the tournament, with Wilson overcoming several tough opponents to reach the final.
