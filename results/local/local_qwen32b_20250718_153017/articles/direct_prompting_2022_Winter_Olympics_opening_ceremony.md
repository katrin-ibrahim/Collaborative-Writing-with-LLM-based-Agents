# 2022_Winter_Olympics_opening_ceremony

### Parade of Nations

The lighting of the Olympic cauldron is one of the most iconic moments of any Olympic opening ceremony. In 2022, the cauldron was lit by four-time Olympic gold medalist Zhao Hongbo and his wife Shen Xue, both former figure skaters who won gold in the pairs event at the 2010 Winter Olympics.

### Olympic Oath

The Olympic oath was taken by two Chinese athletes: Gu Ailing, a freestyle skier, and Ren Ziwei, a short track speed skater. They represented all athletes competing in the games and pledged to compete fairly and respect the rules.
